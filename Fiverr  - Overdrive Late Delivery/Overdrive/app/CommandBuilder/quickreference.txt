This is the down-and-dirty guide for new users to get started successfully using Overdrive.  

To type text on the screen:
Send, Hello World!

To press enter:
Send, {enter}

To wait 1 second:
Sleep, 1000

To open a dot-phrase in a blank note:
Send, ^+n
Sleep, 1000
Send, .mydotphrase{enter}

! = alt
^ = control
+ = shift
